DSP-3431 GRANITE
Create Typed DTOs for endpoints
Granite SWAT
5 Point(s)

At present, 2 endpoints do not use strongly typed DTO models for their result data. This creates a problem, in that the entities should specify the data contract.

We are unable to verify endpoint data contracts at compile time. This also makes tracking schema/data contract changes through source control among multiple teams much harder.

This includes the 'Bills' and 'Usage' endpoints
